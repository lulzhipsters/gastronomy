# Pad Kra Pao

Still very much a work-in-progress. Method and exact amounts TBC.

## Ingredients
Per serve

- Pork or chicken mince
- Garlic
- Fish sauce
- White pepper
- Dark soy sauce (or substitute keycap manis) 
- Light soy sauce
- Oyster sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- Holy basil (or substitute sweet basil)
- Red chillies (birdseye if you like it hot)
- 1 egg
- Jasmine rice
